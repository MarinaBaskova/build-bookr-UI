About Bookr

Bookr was created by six self-learners who wanted a better place to find, rate, and review the best textbooks on any subject. They were tired of having to google for textbooks about the subjec they were studying and having to sift through numerous sites figuring out which textbooks are worth studying. After finding a textbook, figuring out where to purchase or borrow it at the best possible price was another hurdle. Thus, Bookr was born.
